Product Overview

Admon is a premium botanical biopesticide formulated with a synergistic blend of potent essential oils. This advanced formulation provides effective control against a wide range of insect pests while being completely safe for beneficial insects, pollinators, and the environment.

With its unique mode of action and rapid knockdown effect, Admon offers an excellent solution for resistance management in integrated pest management (IPM) programs. Its zero-day pre-harvest interval makes it ideal for use right up to harvest day.

Recommended Dosage

Target Pest Crop Type Dosage per Acre Application Frequency
Aphids, Whiteflies Vegetables, Fruits 300-500 ml / acre 7-10 day intervals
Thrips, Mites All crops 400-600 ml / acre 5-7 day intervals
Mealybugs, Scales Fruits, Ornamentals 500-750 ml / acre 10-14 day intervals
Preventive Application All crops 200-300 ml / acre 14 day intervals

Application Guidelines: Apply as foliar spray with thorough coverage of both sides of leaves. Use higher dosage for heavy infestations. Can be tank-mixed with other compatible biopesticides. Avoid application during peak sunlight hours.

Mode of Action

Admon's unique essential oil blend works through multiple mechanisms to control pest populations effectively:

Respiratory Disruption: Essential oils interfere with insect respiration by blocking spiracles or disrupting respiratory enzymes, leading to suffocation.

Nervous System Impact: Certain oil components affect the insect nervous system, causing paralysis and eventual death.

Growth Regulation: Components like azadirachtin from neem oil disrupt molting and reproduction cycles.

Repellent Action: Strong aromatic compounds deter pests from feeding and oviposition on treated plants.

Cuticle Penetration: Essential oils can dissolve or disrupt the waxy cuticle of insects, leading to dehydration.

This multi-target approach makes Admon highly effective while minimizing the risk of resistance development.
