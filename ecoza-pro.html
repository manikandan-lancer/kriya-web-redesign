ECOZAPRO

Low-Dosage Azadirachtin Solution

Cost-effective pest management for routine IPM programs and preventive applications

Azadirachtin 0.3% EC IPM Optimized Cost-Effective Preventive Care

Product Overview

Ecoza Pro is a carefully formulated 0.3% azadirachtin EC designed specifically for routine Integrated Pest Management programs and preventive applications. This low-dosage formulation provides effective pest control while optimizing cost-efficiency for growers.

Ideal for regular maintenance sprays and preventive pest management strategies, Ecoza Pro delivers consistent performance with minimal environmental impact, making it perfect for sustainable farming operations and organic production systems.

Recommended Dosage

Application Purpose Dosage per Acre Application Frequency
Preventive Application 250 ml - 400 ml / acre 10-14 day intervals
Light Infestation 400 ml - 600 ml / acre 7-10 day intervals
Maintenance Program 300 ml - 500 ml / acre Weekly to bi-weekly
Nursery & Seedlings 200 ml - 350 ml / acre As needed, 10-14 day intervals

Application Guidelines: Dilution: 1–2 ml per litre of water. Apply as foliar spray ensuring complete coverage. For preventive programs, begin applications before pest pressure builds. Can be tank-mixed with other compatible IPM inputs.

Mode of Action

Ecoza Pro utilizes the proven insect growth regulation properties of azadirachtin at an optimized concentration for preventive pest management. The 0.3% EC formulation provides consistent, reliable control while maintaining cost-effectiveness.

As an insect growth regulator, azadirachtin disrupts the molting process of immature insects, preventing them from developing into reproductive adults. This breaks the pest lifecycle and prevents population explosions.

The low concentration is particularly effective for maintaining pest populations below economic threshold levels, making Ecoza Pro an ideal choice for integrated pest management programs where prevention is prioritized over eradication.
