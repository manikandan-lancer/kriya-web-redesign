Benefits of Humic-Based Biostimulants

Envicta humic products deliver multiple benefits that transform soil health and plant performance through natural organic processes.

🌱

Enhanced Root Development

Stimulates root growth and branching, increasing the plant's ability to absorb water and nutrients from the soil.

🔄

Improved Nutrient Uptake

Chelates essential nutrients, making them more available and easily absorbed by plant roots.

🏗️

Better Soil Structure

Improves soil aggregation, aeration, and water retention capacity while reducing compaction.

🛡️

Stress Resistance

Enhances plant resilience to drought, salinity, and temperature extremes through improved metabolic functions.

Soil Health Transformation

Envicta works comprehensively to improve soil biological, chemical, and physical properties for sustainable agriculture.

🦠

Biological Enhancement

Stimulates beneficial microbial activity in the rhizosphere, creating a thriving soil ecosystem that supports plant growth.

🧪

Chemical Optimization

Buffers soil pH, increases cation exchange capacity (CEC), and reduces nutrient leaching for better fertilizer efficiency.

🏔️

Physical Improvement

Enhances soil structure, porosity, and water infiltration while reducing surface crusting and erosion.

Application Recommendations

Optimal application methods and rates for different crop systems and soil conditions.

Application Method Crop Type Recommended Rate Application Timing
Soil Application Field Crops, Vegetables 5-10 kg/acre Pre-planting or during land preparation
Fertigation All irrigated crops 2-3 kg/acre Every 15-20 days during growth period
Foliar Spray Horticultural crops 1-2 g/L water Critical growth stages
Seed Treatment All crops 5-10 g/kg seeds Before sowing
