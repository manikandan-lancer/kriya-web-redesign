About MargoSpin

MargoSpin represents a breakthrough in integrated pest management, combining the proven efficacy of neem-based compounds with the powerful action of spinosad. This synergistic combination offers multiple modes of action, reducing the risk of pest resistance while providing comprehensive protection.

Key Benefits

  • Dual-action mechanism: antifeedant + contact action
  • Reduced risk of pest resistance development
  • Safe for beneficial insects and pollinators
  • Broad-spectrum control of chewing and sucking pests
  • Compatible with IPM programs
  • Short pre-harvest interval

Technical Specifications

Active Ingredients Azadirachtin + Spinosad
Formulation Suspension Concentrate (SC)
Target Pests Thrips, Caterpillars, Leaf miners, Aphids, Whiteflies
Recommended Crops Vegetables, Fruits, Cereals, Cotton, Soybean
Dosage 1-2 ml per liter of water
