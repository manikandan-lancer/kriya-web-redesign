MargoSpin

Advanced neem oil and spinosad combination product for superior pest control in organic agriculture.

MargoSpin Product Details

eco

MargoSpin combines the natural insect-repelling properties of neem oil with the powerful insecticidal action of spinosad for a comprehensive pest management solution.

science

Our unique formulation leverages the synergistic effects of both active ingredients, providing both immediate knockdown and long-term protection against a wide range of pests.

pest_control

MargoSpin effectively controls lepidopteran pests, sucking insects, mites, and other common agricultural pests through multiple modes of action.

settings

Neem oil acts as an antifeedant and growth disruptor, while spinosad provides rapid neurotoxic action, creating a powerful dual-defense system.

public

MargoSpin is biodegradable, safe for beneficial insects when used as directed, and approved for use in organic farming systems worldwide.

Application Guidelines

tips_and_updates

Dosage: Use 1.0-2.0 ml per liter of water depending on pest pressure and crop stage.

schedule

Timing: Apply at first sign of pest infestation or as a preventive measure during vulnerable crop stages.

water_drop

Spray Volume: Ensure thorough coverage of all plant parts, especially the undersides of leaves.

warning

Precautions: Avoid application during peak pollinator activity. Do not apply in extreme temperatures.

rotate_right

Rotation: Can be used in rotation with other biopesticides for resistance management.
