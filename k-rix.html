Product Overview

K-Rix is a premium botanical fungicide formulated with a synergistic blend of plant extracts known for their antifungal properties. This innovative product provides both protective and curative action against a wide range of fungal diseases affecting various crops.

With its unique dual-action mechanism, K-Rix disrupts fungal cell membranes while stimulating the plant's natural defense systems, offering comprehensive disease management that is safe for beneficial microorganisms and the environment.

Recommended Dosage

Crop Category Dosage per Acre Application Interval
Vegetables & Fruits 400 ml - 600 ml / acre 10-14 days
Field Crops 500 ml - 700 ml / acre 12-15 days
High Disease Pressure 600 ml - 800 ml / acre 7-10 days
Preventive Application 300 ml - 500 ml / acre 14-21 days

Application Guidelines: Apply as foliar spray with thorough coverage of all plant parts. For best results, apply at first sign of disease or as a preventive measure. Use higher rates and shorter intervals under high disease pressure. Compatible with most organic inputs.

Mode of Action

K-Rix employs a sophisticated dual-action mechanism that makes it highly effective against fungal pathogens. The formulation contains specific plant compounds that disrupt fungal cell membranes, leading to leakage of cellular contents and eventual death of the pathogen.

Simultaneously, K-Rix stimulates the plant's natural defense mechanisms, enhancing the production of phytoalexins and other defense-related compounds. This systemic acquired resistance provides long-lasting protection against subsequent infections.

This unique combination of direct antifungal activity and plant defense induction ensures comprehensive disease control while minimizing the risk of resistance development, making K-Rix an invaluable tool in sustainable crop protection programs.
