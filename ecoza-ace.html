Azadirachtin 1.2% EC Enhanced Formula Synergistic Action Quick Knockdown

Product Overview

Ecoza Ace represents the next generation in neem-based biopesticides, featuring an enhanced formulation with synergistic botanical extracts that amplify the efficacy of standardized azadirachtin fractions.

This advanced 1.2% EC formulation delivers rapid knockdown combined with prolonged residual activity, making it ideal for challenging pest situations while maintaining the eco-friendly profile that distinguishes the Ecoza product line.

Key Features

Enhanced Formula

Combines azadirachtin with synergistic botanical extracts for amplified pest control efficacy.

Rapid Action

Quick knockdown effect combined with sustained residual protection against pest reinfestation.

Broad-Spectrum Control

Effective against resistant strains of aphids, whiteflies, thrips, and other challenging pests.

Enhanced Penetration

Advanced formulation ensures better plant surface coverage and penetration for maximum efficacy.

Crop Safety

Gentle on crops while tough on pests, suitable for use on sensitive and high-value crops.

Recommended Dosage

Crop Type Dosage per Acre Application Frequency
Vegetables & Fruits 250 ml - 500 ml / acre 7-10 day intervals
Field Crops 300 ml - 600 ml / acre 10-14 day intervals
Heavy Infestation 500 ml - 750 ml / acre 5-7 day intervals initially
Preventive Application 200 ml - 350 ml / acre 14 day intervals

Application Guidelines: Dilution: 1–2 ml per litre of water. Apply as foliar spray with thorough coverage of both sides of leaves. For best results, apply during early morning or late afternoon. Can be used in combination with other compatible biocontrol agents.

Mode of Action

Ecoza Ace's enhanced formula combines the proven insect growth regulation properties of azadirachtin with complementary botanical extracts that provide immediate repellent and antifeedant effects.

The synergistic action disrupts multiple physiological processes in pests simultaneously - inhibiting molting, reducing feeding activity, repelling egg-laying, and interfering with reproduction cycles.

This multi-target approach not only provides rapid control of existing infestations but also creates protective barriers that deter future pest attacks, making Ecoza Ace particularly effective in resistance management programs.
