Mycova Product Details

eco

Mycova is a premium bioinsecticide based on Beauveria bassiana, a naturally occurring entomopathogenic fungus that effectively controls a wide range of insect pests.

science

Our advanced formulations ensure high viability and efficacy of fungal spores, providing reliable pest control through natural infection processes.

pest_control

Mycova targets pests including whiteflies, aphids, thrips, mealybugs, and various caterpillar species through fungal infection and colonization.

settings

The fungus infects pests by penetrating their cuticle, growing inside the insect body, and eventually causing death while producing new spores.

public

Mycova is completely safe for beneficial insects, pollinators, and the environment, making it ideal for integrated pest management programs.

Application Guidelines

tips_and_updates

Dosage: Use 2.0-3.0 ml per liter for Mycova EC and 3.0-4.0 g per liter for Mycova WP depending on pest pressure.

schedule

Timing: Apply during early morning or late evening when humidity is high for optimal fungal activity.

water_drop

Spray Volume: Ensure thorough coverage of both upper and lower leaf surfaces where pests are present.

warning

Precautions: Avoid mixing with fungicides. Maintain adequate humidity for 8-12 hours after application.

rotate_right

Rotation: Can be used in rotation with other biopesticides for integrated pest management.
