Product Overview

Seira EC is a premium emulsifiable concentrate formulation containing Verticillium lecanii, a highly effective entomopathogenic fungus specifically targeting sucking pests. This advanced EC formulation ensures excellent dispersion, easy handling, and superior performance in various agricultural systems.

Verticillium lecanii in Seira EC exhibits exceptional pathogenicity against aphids, whiteflies, thrips, and other sucking insects, providing comprehensive pest control while maintaining environmental safety and compatibility with beneficial insects. Ideal for integrated pest management programs in both conventional and organic farming systems.

Application Guidelines: Shake well before use. Prepare emulsion in clean water and use within 4-6 hours. Apply during evening hours or cloudy conditions. Ensure thorough coverage of both leaf surfaces. Repeat applications at 7-10 day intervals based on pest pressure. Avoid application during peak sunlight hours.

Mode of Action

Seira EC employs sophisticated biological mechanisms that make Verticillium lecanii highly effective against sucking pests:

Contact Infection: Fungal spores come into direct contact with target insects, germinate on the cuticle, and penetrate the insect body through enzymatic action and mechanical pressure.

Cuticle Penetration: Produces cuticle-degrading enzymes (chitinases, proteases, lipases) that break down the insect's protective outer layer, allowing fungal hyphae to enter the body cavity.

Internal Colonization: Once inside the insect, the fungus proliferates throughout the body, consuming internal tissues and nutrients, leading to insect mortality within 3-7 days.

Sporulation: After the insect dies, the fungus grows out through the cuticle and produces new spores that can infect additional pests, creating a self-sustaining control cycle.

Secondary Infection: New spores are released into the environment, providing ongoing protection and infection of subsequent pest generations.
