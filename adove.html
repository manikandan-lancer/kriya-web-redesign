Advanced Botanical Insecticide

Plant-based solution for effective and eco-friendly insect pest management

Botanical formulation Broad-spectrum control Fast-acting Residue-free

Product Overview

Admira Adove is a premium botanical insecticide derived from carefully selected plant extracts with proven insecticidal properties. Its unique formulation provides rapid knockdown and residual control of a wide range of insect pests.

With its plant-based composition and eco-friendly profile, Admira Adove offers an effective alternative to synthetic insecticides while maintaining crop safety and environmental compatibility. Ideal for integrated pest management programs in conventional and organic farming systems.

Key Features

Botanical Origin

Derived from natural plant extracts with proven insecticidal properties, ensuring environmental compatibility.

Fast Action

Provides rapid knockdown of target pests with visible results within hours of application.

Broad-Spectrum Control

Effective against sucking pests, chewing insects, and mites across multiple crop types.

IPM Compatibility

Integrates seamlessly with biological control agents and other IPM components.

Zero Residue

Leaves no harmful residues, making it ideal for pre-harvest applications and export-quality produce.

Target Pests

Aphids

Effective control of various aphid species including green peach aphid and cotton aphid.

Whiteflies

Rapid knockdown and control of whitefly populations in multiple crops.

Thrips

Manages thrips infestations in vegetables, fruits, and ornamental crops.

Leafhoppers

Controls leafhopper species that transmit viral diseases in crops.

Mealybugs

Effective against various mealybug species in fruits and greenhouse crops.

Mites

Suppresses spider mite and other mite populations in multiple cropping systems.

Recommended Dosage

Crop Category Dosage per Acre Application Interval
Vegetables 400-500 ml / acre 7-10 days or as needed
Fruits 450-600 ml / acre 10-14 days during pest season
Field Crops 350-500 ml / acre 10-12 days based on monitoring
Ornamentals 400-550 ml / acre 7-14 days as preventive or curative

Application Guidelines: Apply as foliar spray with thorough coverage of both leaf surfaces. Use higher dosage for heavy infestations or dense canopy. Apply during cooler parts of the day for optimal efficacy. Can be used in tank mixes with compatible products.

Mode of Action

Admira Adove works through multiple modes of action that target the nervous system and physiological processes of insect pests.

The active botanical compounds interfere with neurotransmitter function, causing rapid paralysis and knockdown of target insects. Additional mechanisms include disruption of feeding behavior and inhibition of egg-laying activities.

This multi-pronged approach ensures effective pest control while minimizing the risk of resistance development. The natural compounds break down rapidly in the environment, leaving no persistent residues.

Admira Adove's selective action preserves beneficial insect populations, making it an ideal component of sustainable agriculture practices and integrated pest management programs.
