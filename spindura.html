Spindura Product Details

eco

Spindura is a premium biopesticide based on spinosad, a naturally occurring compound derived from soil bacteria Saccharopolyspora spinosa.

science

Our spinosad formulations are developed through advanced fermentation technology to ensure consistent quality and efficacy against target pests.

pest_control

Spindura effectively controls lepidopteran pests, thrips, leafminers, and other insects through unique neurotoxic action.

settings

Its mode of action involves activation of nicotinic acetylcholine receptors, causing rapid excitation of the insect nervous system.

public

Spindura is biodegradable, has minimal impact on beneficial insects, and is approved for use in organic farming systems.
