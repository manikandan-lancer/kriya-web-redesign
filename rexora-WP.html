Product Overview

Rexora WP is a premium bioinsecticide formulated with Metarhizium anisopliae, a highly effective entomopathogenic fungus that naturally controls a wide range of insect pests. This wettable powder formulation contains 1x10⁹ CFU/g of viable spores for maximum efficacy.

Rexora WP works through fungal infection, penetrating the insect cuticle and proliferating inside the host body. This biological control method provides sustainable pest management while being completely safe for humans, animals, and beneficial insects.

Mode of Action

Metarhizium anisopliae in Rexora WP acts through a sophisticated biological process that ensures effective pest control:

Contact and Infection: Fungal spores contact the insect cuticle, germinate, and penetrate using a combination of mechanical pressure and cuticle-degrading enzymes (proteases, chitinases).

Internal Colonization: Once inside the insect hemocoel, the fungus proliferates, consuming nutrients and producing secondary metabolites that weaken the host.

Host Death: The insect typically dies within 3-7 days post-infection due to nutrient depletion, tissue damage, and fungal toxins.

Sporulation: After host death, the fungus emerges through the cuticle and produces new spores that can infect additional insects, creating a self-sustaining control cycle.

This biological control method is highly specific to target pests and poses no risk to non-target organisms, making it ideal for sustainable agriculture.
