WeedX

Advanced MCT-based natural herbicide for effective and eco-friendly weed control in agriculture.

WeedX Product Details

eco

WeedX is a revolutionary natural herbicide based on Medium Chain Triglycerides (MCT) that provides effective weed control while being environmentally safe.

science

Our unique MCT formulation disrupts weed cell membranes, causing rapid desiccation and control of both broadleaf weeds and grasses.

pest_control

WeedX effectively controls a wide range of common weeds including lambsquarters, pigweed, purslane, and many grass species.

settings

The MCT technology works by dissolving the waxy cuticle of weed leaves, leading to rapid dehydration and plant death within hours.

public

WeedX is biodegradable, has no soil residual activity, and is safe for use in organic farming systems and sensitive environments.

Product Specifications

Parameter Specification
Active Ingredient Medium Chain Triglycerides (MCT)
Formulation Emulsifiable Concentrate (EC)
Color Clear to Pale Yellow
Odor Characteristic
Target Weeds Broadleaf weeds, Grasses, Annual weeds
Recommended Crops All field crops, vegetables, fruits, ornamentals
Dosage 2.0-4.0 ml per liter of water
Application Timing Post-emergence when weeds are actively growing
Rainfastness 2-4 hours after application
