About WeedX

WeedX represents a breakthrough in natural weed management, utilizing Medium Chain Triglycerides (MCT) technology to provide effective, eco-friendly weed control. This innovative approach disrupts weed cell membranes while being safe for crops, soil, and the environment.

Key Benefits

  • Fast-acting contact herbicide action
  • No soil residue or persistence
  • Safe for subsequent crop planting
  • Biodegradable and environmentally friendly
  • Effective against both broadleaf and grassy weeds
  • No weed resistance development
  • Suitable for organic farming systems

Technical Specifications

Active Ingredient Medium Chain Triglycerides (MCT)
Formulation Emulsifiable Concentrate (EC)
Target Weeds Broadleaf weeds, Grasses, Annual weeds
Recommended Crops All field crops, Vegetables, Orchards
Application Rate 2-4 liters per hectare
Rainfastness 2-4 hours after application

Mode of Action

WeedX works by disrupting the cell membranes of weed foliage upon contact. The MCT compounds penetrate the weed's protective layers, causing rapid desiccation and cell death. This physical mode of action prevents weed resistance development and ensures consistent performance.
