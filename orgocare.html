Comprehensive Stress Management

Orgocare products provide multi-layered protection against various abiotic stresses that impact crop growth and yield potential.

🌡️

Temperature Stress

Protects against both heat and cold stress by stabilizing cellular membranes and enhancing thermotolerance.

💧

Water Stress

Improves water use efficiency and maintains turgor pressure during drought or waterlogging conditions.

☀️

Radiation Stress

Shields plants from UV radiation and high light intensity by enhancing antioxidant systems.

🧂

Salinity Stress

Mitigates salt stress effects by regulating ion homeostasis and osmotic balance in plant cells.

How Orgocare Works

Orgocare activates multiple defense mechanisms within plants to build resilience against environmental stresses.

1

Activation of Defense Genes

Triggers expression of stress-responsive genes that encode for protective proteins and enzymes, preparing plants for potential stress events.

2

Antioxidant System Enhancement

Boosts production of antioxidants like glutathione, ascorbate, and catalase that neutralize reactive oxygen species generated during stress.

3

Osmoprotectant Accumulation

Stimulates synthesis of compatible solutes like proline and glycine betaine that maintain cellular water balance and protect macromolecules.

4

Hormonal Balance Regulation

Modulates plant hormone levels, particularly ABA and cytokinins, to optimize stress responses while maintaining growth processes.

Application Recommendations

Optimal application timing and methods for maximum stress protection across different crop types.

Crop Type Preventive Application Curative Application Recommended Rate
Cereals (Wheat, Rice, Corn) Tillering stage / Before stress season At first signs of stress symptoms 1-2 ml/L water
Vegetables Transplanting / Early vegetative stage Immediately when stress is anticipated 1.5-2 ml/L water
Fruit Crops Bud break / Fruit set stage During extreme weather forecasts 2-3 ml/L water
Cash Crops (Cotton, Sugarcane) Early growth stages During drought or heat waves 1.5-2.5 ml/L water
Horticultural Crops Active growth phase Before expected stress events 2-3 ml/L water
