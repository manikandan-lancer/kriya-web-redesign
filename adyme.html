ADYME

Advanced Systemic Fungicide

Professional-grade fungicide with dual-action formula for superior disease control

Dual-action formula Systemic & curative Broad-spectrum control Crop safety

Product Overview

Adyme is a premium systemic fungicide with a unique dual-action formula that provides both preventive and curative control of a wide range of fungal diseases. Its advanced formulation ensures rapid uptake and translocation throughout the plant.

With excellent crop safety and flexible application timing, Adyme fits seamlessly into modern crop protection programs, offering reliable disease management for enhanced yield potential and crop quality.

Key Features

Dual-Action Formula

Combines preventive and curative activity for comprehensive disease control at different infection stages.

Systemic Activity

Rapidly absorbed and translocated throughout plant tissues, providing protection to new growth.

Broad-Spectrum Control

Effective against a wide range of fungal pathogens including powdery mildew, rusts, leaf spots, and blights.

Excellent Crop Safety

Well-tolerated by most crops with minimal phytotoxicity risk when used according to label directions.

Resistance Management

Multi-site activity helps prevent resistance development, extending product efficacy.

Recommended Dosage

Crop Dosage per Acre Application Timing
Cereals 200-300 ml / acre At disease onset or as preventive spray
Vegetables 250-400 ml / acre 7-14 day intervals depending on disease pressure
Fruits 300-500 ml / acre Pre-bloom to fruit development stages
Ornamentals 200-350 ml / acre As needed based on disease monitoring

Application Guidelines: Apply as foliar spray with thorough coverage. Use sufficient water volume for complete coverage. Begin applications before disease development or at first sign of infection. Repeat at 7-14 day intervals as needed.

Mode of Action

Adyme works through a unique dual-action mechanism that combines preventive and curative activity against fungal pathogens.

Its systemic properties allow for rapid uptake and translocation throughout plant tissues, protecting both treated and new growth from fungal infections.

The multi-site activity interferes with multiple metabolic processes in fungal cells, providing effective control while minimizing the risk of resistance development.

Adyme inhibits spore germination and mycelial growth, disrupting the fungal life cycle at multiple stages for comprehensive disease management.
