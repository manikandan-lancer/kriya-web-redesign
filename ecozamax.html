Product Overview

Ecoza is a premium neem-based biopesticide formulated with standardized azadirachtin fractions. Available in multiple strengths and convenient formulations, Ecoza provides broad-spectrum control against sucking and chewing pests.

Its eco-friendly profile allows distributors to position it as a residue-conscious, organic alternative to synthetic insecticides. With quick knockdown and systemic activity, Ecoza fits seamlessly into integrated pest management (IPM) programs worldwide.

Key Features

Natural Composition

Derived from premium neem seed extracts with standardized 3.0% azadirachtin content for consistent performance.

Broad-Spectrum Efficacy

Effectively controls aphids, whiteflies, thrips, mealybugs, caterpillars, and other sucking and chewing pests.

Eco-Friendly

Biodegradable formulation that's safe for beneficial insects, pollinators, and the environment when used as directed.

Multiple Modes of Action

Works as insect growth regulator, antifeedant, and repellent to prevent pest resistance development.

IPM Compatibility

Ideal for Integrated Pest Management programs with no harmful residues and short pre-harvest intervals.

Recommended Dosage

Product Variant Dosage per Acre Dilution per Liter
Ecoza Max (EC 3.0%) 200 ml - 450 ml / acre 1–2 ml per litre of water

Application Guidelines: Apply as foliar spray with thorough coverage of both sides of leaves. Repeat at 7-10 day intervals or as needed based on pest pressure. Use higher dosage for heavy infestations.

Mode of Action

Azadirachtin, the active ingredient in EcozaMax, acts as a potent insect growth regulator (IGR) that disrupts the molting process and reproduction cycle of target pests.

It also functions as an antifeedant, making treated plants unpalatable to pests, and as a repellent, deterring insects from laying eggs on treated surfaces.

This multi-pronged approach effectively controls pest populations while minimizing the risk of resistance development, making EcozaMax an ideal component of sustainable agriculture practices.
