Product Overview

Margoshine 70% is a highly concentrated neem-based biopesticide containing 70% Azadirachtin, the most potent bioactive compound derived from neem seeds. This premium formulation offers superior pest control through multiple modes of action while being completely safe for beneficial insects and the environment.

With its high concentration of Azadirachtin, Margoshine 70% provides exceptional efficacy against a wide range of agricultural pests at lower application rates, making it both cost-effective and environmentally responsible for modern sustainable agriculture.

Mode of Action

Azadirachtin, the active compound in Margoshine 70%, exhibits multiple modes of action that make it highly effective against a broad spectrum of agricultural pests. Its primary action is as a potent antifeedant, blocking the insects' ability to recognize host plants and stopping feeding activity within hours of application.

Additionally, Azadirachtin acts as a powerful insect growth regulator by mimicking insect hormones and disrupting the molting process. This prevents larvae from developing into adults and stops reproduction in adult insects. It also exhibits repellent properties that deter pests from settling on treated plants.

This multi-pronged approach ensures comprehensive pest control while minimizing the risk of resistance development. Unlike synthetic pesticides that typically have a single mode of action, Azadirachtin's complex mechanism makes it extremely difficult for pests to develop resistance.
