Key Benefits of Enrhize

Enrhize mycorrhizal products establish symbiotic relationships with plant roots, creating extensive fungal networks that dramatically improve plant health and productivity.

🌱

Enhanced Root Development

Increases root surface area by up to 700%, improving water and nutrient absorption.

💧

Improved Drought Tolerance

Mycorrhizal networks extend beyond root zones, accessing water from larger soil volumes.

🌿

Better Nutrient Uptake

Enhances phosphorus, zinc, copper and other micronutrient absorption significantly.

🛡️

Disease Resistance

Creates physical and biochemical barriers against root pathogens and soil-borne diseases.

Application Guide

Follow these guidelines for optimal results with Enrhize mycorrhizal products across different crop types.

Crop Type Application Method Recommended Rate Application Timing
Field Crops In-furrow application or seed treatment 2-4 kg/hectare At planting or transplanting
Vegetables Root dip or in planting hole 1-2 g/plant During transplanting
Fruit Trees Soil drench around root zone 50-100 g/tree Early spring or during planting
Ornamentals Mix in potting soil or root dip 1-2 g/L of soil At planting or repotting
Turf & Lawns Broadcast and water in 5-10 kg/hectare Spring or fall establishment
