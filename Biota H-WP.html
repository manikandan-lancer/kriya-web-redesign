BIOTAH-WP

Trichoderma harzianum Wettable Powder

Advanced wettable powder formulation featuring the most aggressive biocontrol strain

2x10^9 CFU/g Wettable Powder Aggressive biocontrol Organic certified

Product Overview

Biota H-WP is a premium wettable powder formulation containing a high concentration of Trichoderma harzianum, widely recognized as one of the most aggressive and effective biocontrol agents against soil-borne fungal pathogens. This advanced WP formulation combines the superior antagonistic properties of T. harzianum with the practical advantages of wettable powder technology.

The formulation ensures excellent suspension properties, easy handling, and superior performance across various agricultural systems. Biota H-WP provides comprehensive protection against a wide spectrum of soil-borne diseases while actively promoting plant growth and enhancing soil health through sustainable biological means.

Key Features

Advanced WP Technology

Wettable powder formulation ensures excellent suspension, easy mixing, and uniform application in water-based systems.

Aggressive Biocontrol

Trichoderma harzianum exhibits exceptional mycoparasitic activity against a broad range of fungal pathogens.

Rapid Colonization

Quick establishment in the rhizosphere with superior competitive ability against pathogenic fungi.

Growth Promotion

Enhances root development, nutrient uptake, and overall plant vigor through multiple mechanisms.

Soil Health Enhancement

Improves soil structure, enhances microbial diversity, and promotes sustainable agricultural ecosystems.

Recommended Dosage

Application Method Dosage Water Volume
Seed Treatment 5-10 g per kg seeds Make slurry with 100-200 ml water
Soil Drenching 2.5-5 kg per acre 500-1000 liters water per acre
Nursery Treatment 2-3 kg per cubic meter Mix with soil or apply as drench
Drip Irrigation 1-2 kg per acre Apply through drip system
Furrow Application 2-4 kg per acre Mix with 50-100 kg FYM/compost

Application Guidelines: Prepare suspension in clean water and use within 4-6 hours. Avoid mixing with chemical fungicides. Apply during cooler parts of the day. Maintain adequate soil moisture for optimal establishment. Store in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Mode of Action

Biota H-WP employs multiple sophisticated biological mechanisms that make Trichoderma harzianum one of the most effective biocontrol agents available:

Aggressive Mycoparasitism: T. harzianum exhibits exceptional mycoparasitic activity, directly attacking pathogenic fungi through hyphal coiling, penetration, and secretion of powerful cell wall-degrading enzymes including chitinases, glucanases, and proteases.

Superior Competition: Competes aggressively with pathogens for space, nutrients, and infection sites in the rhizosphere through exceptional rhizosphere competence and rapid colonization ability.

Multiple Antibiosis: Produces a wide spectrum of antibiotics and secondary metabolites (trichodermin, harzianic acid, gliovirin, harzianopyridone) that effectively inhibit pathogenic fungi.

Induced Systemic Resistance: Activates the plant's natural defense mechanisms, priming it for enhanced resistance to subsequent pathogen attacks through multiple signaling pathways.

Comprehensive Growth Promotion: Produces plant growth hormones, solubilizes nutrients, improves root architecture, and enhances overall plant vigor and productivity.
