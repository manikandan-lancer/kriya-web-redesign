Neuvita Product Details

eco

Neuvita is a powerful biofungicide containing Ampelomyces quisqualis, a hyperparasitic fungus specifically effective against powdery mildew pathogens.

science

Available in both EC and WP formulations, Neuvita provides flexible application options for different crop protection scenarios and farming systems.

pest_control

Highly effective against various powdery mildew species affecting grapes, cucurbits, apples, strawberries, roses, and other susceptible crops.

settings

Mode of action includes mycoparasitism, where Ampelomyces quisqualis penetrates and colonizes powdery mildew fungi, leading to their destruction.

public

Neuvita is an eco-friendly, organic-certifiable solution that's safe for beneficial organisms, pollinators, and the environment.

Mode of Action

1

Contact

Neuvita spores come in contact with powdery mildew mycelium and conidia on plant surfaces.

2

Penetration

Ampelomyces quisqualis penetrates the cell walls of powdery mildew fungi using enzymatic action.

3

Colonization

The hyperparasite grows inside the powdery mildew structures, consuming them as nutrient source.

4

Destruction

Powdery mildew fungi are destroyed, preventing further sporulation and disease spread.

Neuvita Application Guide

Crop Disease Dosage (per acre) Application Interval
Grapes Powdery Mildew 500-750 ml/500-750g 7-10 days
Cucurbits Powdery Mildew 400-600 ml/400-600g 7-10 days
Apples Powdery Mildew 500-750 ml/500-750g 10-14 days
Strawberries Powdery Mildew 400-600 ml/400-600g 7-10 days
Roses & Ornamentals Powdery Mildew 500-750 ml/500-750g 7-10 days

Note: Apply as a preventive measure or at first signs of disease. Maintain good coverage of both leaf surfaces. Avoid application under extreme temperature conditions.
