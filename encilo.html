EnCilo Product Details

eco

EnCilo is a powerful bioinsecticide containing Paecilomyces fumosoroseus, an entomopathogenic fungus highly effective against whiteflies and other sucking pests.

science

Available in both EC and WP formulations, EnCilo provides flexible application options for different agricultural systems and pest management strategies.

pest_control

Highly effective against whiteflies, aphids, thrips, spider mites, and mealybugs in vegetables, fruits, ornamentals, and greenhouse crops.

settings

Mode of action includes spore germination on insect cuticle, penetration through the integument, and growth inside the insect body leading to mortality.

public

EnCilo is an eco-friendly, organic-certifiable solution that's safe for beneficial insects, pollinators, and the environment.

Key Features of EnCilo

verified

High Efficacy

Proven effectiveness against resistant whitefly populations and other difficult-to-control sucking pests.

safety_check

Environmentally Safe

Non-toxic to beneficial insects, pollinators, and mammals. Safe for use in organic farming systems.

psychiatry

Multiple Action

Acts through contact, ingestion, and secondary infection, providing comprehensive pest control.

agriculture

Safe for use on a wide range of crops including vegetables, fruits, ornamentals, and field crops.

EnCilo Application Guide

Crop Target Pest Dosage (per acre) Application Interval
Tomato, Cucumber Whiteflies, Aphids 500-750 ml/500-750g 7-10 days
Cotton, Soybean Whiteflies, Thrips 750-1000 ml/750-1000g 10-14 days
Ornamentals Whiteflies, Mealybugs 500-750 ml/500-750g 7-10 days
Greenhouse crops Whiteflies, Spider mites 750-1000 ml/750-1000g 7 days
Fruit crops Aphids, Thrips 500-750 ml/500-750g 10-14 days

Note: Apply during early morning or late evening for best results. Maintain adequate humidity for optimal fungal activity.
