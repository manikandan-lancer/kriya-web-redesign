Product Overview

Ecoza Rix is a premium water-soluble powder (WSP) formulation of azadirachtin, presented in convenient tablet form for precise and easy application. This innovative delivery system ensures accurate dosing and complete dissolution in water.

With its concentrated 1.2% azadirachtin content, Ecoza Rix provides powerful broad-spectrum control against sucking and chewing pests while maintaining the eco-friendly profile that makes it suitable for organic farming and IPM programs.

Recommended Dosage

Application Dosage Preparation
Standard Application 2–3 tablets per litre of spray solution Dissolve tablets in water before adding to spray tank
Heavy Infestation 3–4 tablets per litre of spray solution Increase concentration for severe pest pressure
Preventive Treatment 1–2 tablets per litre of spray solution Lower dosage for routine preventive applications

Application Guidelines: Dissolve required number of tablets in small amount of water first, then add to spray tank with remaining water. Apply as foliar spray with thorough coverage of both sides of leaves. Repeat at 7-10 day intervals or as needed.

Mode of Action

Azadirachtin, the active ingredient in Ecoza Rix, acts as a potent insect growth regulator (IGR) that disrupts the molting process and reproduction cycle of target pests by interfering with their hormonal systems.

It also functions as an antifeedant, making treated plants unpalatable to pests, and as a repellent, deterring insects from laying eggs on treated surfaces. This multi-pronged approach prevents pest resistance development.

The water-soluble formulation ensures rapid absorption and systemic movement within plant tissues, providing both contact and systemic protection against pest infestations.
