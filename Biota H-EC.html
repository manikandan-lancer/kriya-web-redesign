Trichoderma harzianum Biofungicide

Advanced emulsifiable concentrate for superior biological disease control

2x10^9 CFU/ml Emulsifiable Concentrate Broad-spectrum control Organic certified

Product Overview

Biota H-EC is a premium emulsifiable concentrate formulation containing a high concentration of Trichoderma harzianum, one of the most effective and aggressive biocontrol agents against soil-borne fungal pathogens. This advanced EC formulation ensures excellent dispersion, easy handling, and superior performance in various agricultural systems.

Trichoderma harzianum in Biota H-EC exhibits exceptional rhizosphere competence and multiple antagonistic mechanisms, providing comprehensive protection against a wide range of soil-borne diseases while promoting plant growth and soil health through sustainable biological means.

Mode of Action

Biota H-EC employs multiple sophisticated biological mechanisms that make Trichoderma harzianum one of the most effective biocontrol agents:

Mycoparasitism: Trichoderma harzianum directly attacks and parasitizes fungal pathogens through hyphal coiling, penetration, and secretion of cell wall-degrading enzymes including chitinases, glucanases, and proteases.

Competition: Competes aggressively with pathogens for space, nutrients, and infection sites in the rhizosphere, effectively excluding them from the root zone through superior rhizosphere competence.

Antibiosis: Produces a wide range of antibiotics and secondary metabolites (trichodermin, harzianic acid, gliovirin) that inhibit the growth and development of pathogenic fungi.

Induced Systemic Resistance: Activates the plant's natural defense mechanisms, priming it for enhanced resistance to subsequent pathogen attacks through jasmonic acid and salicylic acid pathways.

Growth Promotion: Produces plant growth hormones (auxins, gibberellins), solubilizes nutrients (phosphorus, micronutrients), and improves root architecture, leading to enhanced plant vigor and productivity.
