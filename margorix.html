MARGORIX

Advanced Neem + Azadirachtin Biopesticide

Dual-mode botanical formulation for rapid, eco-friendly pest suppression and crop safety

Neem + Azadirachtin Blend Broad Spectrum IGR Action Organic Certified

Product Overview

MargoRix is an innovative dual-action biopesticide combining the proven efficacy of neem oil with standardized azadirachtin extracts. This synergy provides superior control of sucking and chewing pests while maintaining excellent crop safety.

Its balanced formulation delivers quick knockdown through neem’s limonoids and prolonged protection via azadirachtin’s hormonal disruption, making MargoRix ideal for Integrated Pest Management (IPM) and residue-free agriculture.

Key Features

Dual Mode Technology

Combines neem oil’s repellency with azadirachtin’s hormonal interference for enhanced pest control.

Eco-Safe Formulation

Derived entirely from botanical sources and suitable for organic cultivation.

Long Residual Protection

Provides sustained activity for up to 10 days after spray under field conditions.

Crop Compatibility

Safe on vegetables, fruits, ornamentals, and plantation crops with no phytotoxic effects.

Resistance Management

Multiple modes of action reduce the risk of pest resistance development.

Biodegradable

Fully biodegradable and environmentally friendly with no soil or water residues.

Benefits of Neem + Azadirachtin

Antifeedant

Stops insect feeding immediately after contact or ingestion.

Repellent

Prevents pest colonization on treated plants.

Growth Disruption

Inhibits molting and metamorphosis of larval stages.

Oviposition Blocker

Prevents egg laying and reduces population build-up.

Target Pests

Sucking Pests

Aphids, Thrips, Whiteflies, Mealybugs, Leafhoppers.

Chewing Pests

Mites & Others

Red Spider Mites, Gall Midges, Stem Borers, Fruit Borers.

Mode of Action

MargoRix operates through a synergistic mechanism combining contact and systemic effects. Neem oil acts as a surface repellent and respiratory inhibitor, while Azadirachtin functions as an Insect Growth Regulator (IGR) and feeding deterrent.

Upon exposure, insects stop feeding and lose the ability to molt or reproduce. This multi-level disruption halts pest cycles effectively without harming pollinators or beneficial arthropods.

Its botanical dual system makes MargoRix an ideal fit for residue-free, sustainable agriculture and export-compliant crop programs.

Mode of Action: Combined neem oil and azadirachtin pathways
