Product Overview

K-Guard is a premium biopesticide formulated with standardized Karanjin extracts derived from Karanja (Pongamia pinnata) seeds. This powerful botanical extract provides effective control against a wide range of agricultural pests through its unique antifeedant and growth disruption properties.

With its 2.0% Karanjin concentration, K-Guard offers reliable pest management while being completely biodegradable and safe for beneficial insects, making it an ideal choice for organic farming and sustainable agriculture practices.

Application Guidelines: Apply as foliar spray with thorough coverage of both sides of leaves. Repeat at 10-15 day intervals depending on pest pressure. For best results, apply during early morning or late evening hours. Compatible with most organic inputs.

Mode of Action

Karanjin, the active compound in K-Guard, exhibits multiple modes of action that make it highly effective against various agricultural pests. Its primary action is as a potent antifeedant, making treated plants unpalatable to pests and stopping feeding activity immediately.

Additionally, Karanjin acts as an insect growth regulator by interfering with the molting process and hormonal balance of insects, preventing proper development and reproduction. It also exhibits repellent properties that deter pests from settling on treated plants.

This multi-pronged approach ensures comprehensive pest control while minimizing the risk of resistance development, making K-Guard an invaluable component of sustainable pest management strategies.
