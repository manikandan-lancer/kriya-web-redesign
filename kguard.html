K-Guard Product Details

eco

K-Guard is a premium botanical insecticide derived from Karanja (Pongamia) oil, standardized for karanjin content for effective pest control.

science

Available in multiple strengths with karanjin concentrations ranging from 2% to 5% in emulsifiable concentrate formulations.

pest_control

The product effectively controls sucking pests like aphids, whiteflies, jassids, and other leaf-eating insects through antifeedant and repellant action.

settings

Its mode of action includes antifeedant properties, repellency, and growth disruption, making it ideal for resistance management.

public

K-Guard is an eco-friendly, biodegradable option suitable for organic farming and integrated pest management programs.
