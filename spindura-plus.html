Product Overview

Spindura Plus is a premium biopesticide formulated with spinosad, a naturally occurring compound derived from the soil bacterium Saccharopolyspora spinosa. This advanced formulation provides exceptional control against lepidopteran pests and other chewing insects.

With its unique mode of action and enhanced rainfast properties, Spindura Plus offers superior performance while maintaining environmental safety. Its selective toxicity makes it ideal for integrated pest management programs where preservation of beneficial insects is crucial.

Mode of Action

Spinosad, the active ingredient in Spindura Plus, acts primarily by affecting the nervous system of target insects through multiple mechanisms:

Nicotinic Acetylcholine Receptors: Spinosad binds to nicotinic acetylcholine receptors in the insect nervous system, causing rapid excitation of neurons.

GABA Receptors: It also affects GABA-gated chloride channels, leading to disruption of normal nerve function and muscle control.

Rapid Knockdown: This dual action results in rapid cessation of feeding, paralysis, and eventual death of target pests within hours of exposure.

Selective Toxicity: The unique biochemical properties of spinosad make it highly effective against target pests while having minimal impact on beneficial insects, mammals, and birds.

This multi-target approach provides effective pest control while minimizing the risk of resistance development.
