Product Overview

Adrlic is a premium selective herbicide specially formulated for effective post-emergence control of broadleaf weeds in various crops. Its advanced formulation ensures targeted action against weeds while maintaining excellent crop safety.

With rapid absorption and translocation, Adrlic provides reliable weed control that enhances crop growth and maximizes yield potential. Its selective mode of action makes it an ideal choice for integrated weed management programs.

Key Features

Selective Action

Targets broadleaf weeds while preserving the health and growth of cereal and other tolerant crops.

Rapid Action

Quickly absorbed by weed foliage with visible symptoms appearing within days of application.

Crop Safety

Excellent safety profile for wheat, barley, corn, and other labeled crops when used as directed.

Systemic Activity

Translocates throughout the weed, ensuring complete control of both above and below-ground parts.

Rainfastness

Becomes rainfast within hours of application, reducing the impact of unexpected rainfall.

Recommended Dosage

Crop Dosage per Acre Application Timing
Wheat & Barley 300-400 ml / acre Post-emergence when weeds are actively growing
Corn 350-450 ml / acre Post-emergence up to 8-leaf stage of corn
Soybeans 400-500 ml / acre Post-emergence before soybean flowering

Application Guidelines: Apply as foliar spray when weeds are small and actively growing (2-4 leaf stage for most annual weeds). Use higher rates for larger weeds or dense infestations. Avoid application under drought stress or extreme temperatures.

Mode of Action

Adrlic works as a systemic herbicide that is rapidly absorbed through weed foliage and translocated throughout the plant.

It inhibits specific enzymes essential for amino acid synthesis in susceptible broadleaf weeds, disrupting protein production and leading to growth cessation and eventual plant death.

The selective action of Adrlic is based on differential metabolism between tolerant crops and susceptible weeds, allowing for effective weed control without harming the crop.
